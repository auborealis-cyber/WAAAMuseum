Events

Our Fun's Over a Century in the Making

WAAAM hosts a variety of events during the year. Including, but not limited to:

  • Second Saturdays- Each month we fly and drive the exhibits. Come and enjoy the fun!
  • WAAAM Traffic Jam Car Show- Rev up the engines and join us for a day filled with old cars of all makes and models
  • Hood River Fly-in- See hundreds of cool planes and talk to pilots too (trust us, they'll tell you who they are)! This event is a great way to learn about aviation on the ground level and experience living history.

Other highlights at WAAAM include class and meeting areas where small groups can get together, a large area inside for special functions, and plenty of space outside for picnics(like our gazebo!), car rallies, and other fun activities!
