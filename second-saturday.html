Second Saturday

We are glad you found us! Every day at WAAAM is a new adventure, especially on second saturdays. The museum collection is housed in over 3.5 acres of indoor hangar facility. Plan to spend at least an hour or two to get an idea of what is here. For the enthusiast, a day can be too short to see all of the cars, planes, motorcycles, military vehicles, and more. For an enthusiast looking for a seat in history, the day can end very soon. Listen to the stories, talk to a docent, recount your own memories and create new ones!

Step back in time with the Western Antique Aeroplane & Automobile Museum.

Please join us while we get the engines running! Take a ride in antique cars and watch our antique planes take off and land.

Join us the Second Saturday of each month when WAAAM gets the engines running. Action usually happens between 10am to 2pm. Lunch is served in the museum between 11am to 1pm.

Second Saturdays

In the winter will be held indoors and weather permitting, cars and airplanes will be taken outside. Otherwise, there will be indoor events, such as demonstrations, special exhibits, and hands-on experience for visitors.

Our antique aircraft, automobiles, motorcycles, tractors, military jeeps, and engines all get their day in the sun when museum volunteers get to show visitors that this is not just piles of dead, decorative metal...but artifacts that still have life yet!

Centenarian Day

From 10am to 2pm, we celebrate the 100th birthday of each item that turned 100 years. Anyone who's interested in seeing what it takes to maintain 100-year-old airplanes can take an Aircraft Restoration Shop Tour at 11am and 1pm. We'll also feature our other vehicles that reached 100, any volunteer would be happy to answer any question on how we keep them alive!

Rides in our automobiles will be available from 10am-2pm, featuring our over 100 year old Detroit Electric!

Local History Spotlights

From 10am to 2pm, rides will be given be given in the automobiles. At 2pm, a talk on the restoration and local history of the featured vehicle will be given. This time it will be on the Shearer Sprayers' SM8-A

Military Vehicles

This will feature our collection of veteran vehicles.

Flying Around the World

This day will feature our planes and vehicles that have been around the world.

Author Talks

We'll have an author who will come in and present their specific passion for aviation/automobile history.

Directions

From Portland

Travel East on I-84. Take Exit 62 and turn right onto Cascade Ave. Turn right at the first traffic signal onto 13th St. 13th St. joins with 12th St. and then becomes Tucker Rd. From there it is only 1.5 miles to Air Museum Rd. The museum is on the right side. Look for the vintage Drive-In theater sign at our entryway. We are right by the airport so think flat open area with no trees
