1972 Honda N600 2-door Sedan

1972 Honda N600 2-door Sedan

Year: 1972

Donation: Phil & Judy Jensen

Engine: 2 Cyl., 36 cubic inches, approx 44 HP

Transmission: Manual (4 speed)

Number of Doors: 2

Curb Weight in Lbs: approx. 1,300

Built to exacting standards for Japanese commuters, the Honda N600 also saw a small spike in popularity in the United States as a cheap but reliable commuter car for those who would rather spend money on adventures, experiences, or themselves.
