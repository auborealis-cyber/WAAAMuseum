We love it when people enjoy our museum so much they want to take a bigger part of it and we're happy to expand your horizons!

Click "Become A Member" to purchase your membership today!

Benefits of Membership

Include, but are not limited to:

  • Unlimited free access to the museum during operation hours
  • Invitations to “Members Only” events
  • 10% discount on Museum Store purchases
  • Access to “Members Only” areas outside museum for pre-flight operations
  • Emails on special events and happenings at the museum
  • Opportunities to become a Museum Volunteer
  • Museum Newsletter

There are several levels of membership available to individuals and families. Participants at each level get to enjoy all of the benefits of membership.

Figure out which membership suits you below:

Student Membership

$25.00 – One named member under 18 years old. Can only be purchased over the phone or in person.

Solo Membership

$75.00 – One named member

Family Membership

$135.00 – One named member & their same household significant other & their kids or grandkids under the age of 18 (6 kid maximum). Comes with 2 bonus single-use admission passes.

Plus Membership

$155.00 – Family Membership benefits plus 1 guest. Comes with 2 bonus single-use admission passes.

Quad Membership

$175.00 – Family Membership benefits plus 4 guests. Comes with 4 bonus single-use admission passes.

Sitter Membership

$25.00 – Must be combined with any level of pass. It allows the card holder to enter under another’s membership when accompanied by the member’s kids. Can only be purchased over the phone or in person.
